This guide will show you how to create your first project and join our community of creators.

<Info>
  **Prerequisite** You should have installed Node.js (version 18.10.0 or
  higher).
</Info>

**1. Sign Up To Eartho Creators**

Create your free Eartho Creators account.\
Go To [**Eartho Creators**](https://creator.eartho.io) and click on connect.

**2. Create Your First Project**

After login, click "**Add Project**" on the main screen. Build anything you can imagine, from websites to apps!

**3. Create Your First Access Point**

Once your project is ready, go to "**Add Access Point**" on the project page. This lets you control how people interact with your project. Do you want them to log in, have a premium option, or something else? Eartho Creators makes it easy to set up.\
\
An access point can be a way for users to log in, subscribe to a premium tier, or gain a license for your project.

**4. Welcome to the Community!**

Congratulations, you've created your first access point! We're excited to have you join the Eartho Creators community.

Come chat with us on our Discord server! It's a great way to meet other creators and get help. We can't wait to see what you build!

It's time to integrate Eartho into your app.
